Mission and Values
Accessibility
Our doors are open. Our center offers the best conditions for partners and clients.
We welcome new employees and volunteers. We are always available
We believe we are PART of a great world of clinical trials
Tuning
We tune ourselves to be open to our partners, and new ideas.
We are ready for experiments, and we know how to find unconventional solutions. We have nothing to hide; we work honestly
Refinement
We learn, we refine our skills, and we broaden our horizons.
We keep up with the latest developments in science and technology and strive to use the most modern and advanced approaches in our work
Person
Our main value is the person. This includes our volunteers, patients, every employee, client, and partner.
We always remember that the well-being and health of volunteers and patients, the confidence of employees, and the satisfaction and peace of mind of clients and partners are our top priorities
